FAQ

How to return an order on Performance Footwear?

Performance Footwear customers can return orders by simply posting them back with a free label provided by us. Please see our return instructions for more information.

Can I return a defective or faulty item?

You may return defective or faulty merchandise with valid proof of purchase within two years from the date of online purchase.

If you have a potentially defective or faulty item and it was delivered within 30 days of purchase, it has a standard return option that suits you best. After 30 days you will need to contact us to begin the return process.
